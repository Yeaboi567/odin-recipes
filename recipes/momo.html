momo

Description

Momo is a nepali dumpling. It is often filled with chicken,buff,goat,vegetables etc. It is mostly served with peanut chutney

Ingredients

  • Minced Meat
  • flour
  • water
  • Onion, cilantro etc

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough Mix all-purpose flour with a pinch of salt and water. Knead until soft and smooth. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Make the filling Mix finely chopped vegetables or minced meat with onion, garlic, ginger, coriander, salt, oil, and momo masala. Adjust spices to taste.
  3. Shape the momos Roll the dough into small thin circles. Add a spoonful of filling to the center. Fold and pleat into desired shapes (half-moon or round).
  4. Steam the momos Place them on a greased steamer tray. Steam for 10â€“12 minutes until the wrapper is slightly transparent and cooked.
