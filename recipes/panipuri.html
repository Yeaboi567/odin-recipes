Pani Puri

Description

Pani-Puri is probably the most famous street snack in the indian subcontinent. They are fried dough balls filled with potato chikpeas and spices. They are dipped in soup and eaten wholly

Ingredients

  • Pani puri dough balls
  • Potatoes, Chickpeas
  • Spices
  • Tamarind chutney

Recipe

  1. Prepare the stuffing Boil and mash potatoes or chickpeas. Add chopped onions, coriander, salt, chili powder, and a squeeze of lemon.
  2. Make the spicy pani Blend mint, coriander, green chilies, ginger, black salt, and tamarind pulp with cold water. Strain and chill the pani.
  3. Assemble and eat Crack the top of each puri, fill with stuffing, pour in the pani, and pop it in your mouth immediately.
