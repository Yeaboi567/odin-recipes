Chaat

Description

Chaat is a savory street food dish from the Indian subcontinent, known for its explosion of flavors sweet, spicy, tangy, and crunchy all at once. Itâ€™s not a single dish but a category of snacks made from a mix of fried or boiled ingredients like potatoes, chickpeas, or puffed rice, tossed with fresh veggies, chutneys, yogurt, and a dash of chaat masala (a special spice mix).

Ingredients

  • chaat masala
  • tamarind chutney
  • green chutney
  • onion
  • potatoes
  • sev
  • chickpeas
  • yogurt
  • herbs and spices

Steps

  1. Boil and chop base ingredients: Boil potatoes (or chickpeas, sprouts, etc.) and chop them into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Chop onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and coriander. Keep tamarind chutney, mint chutney, and curd ready.
  3. In a bowl, combine base ingredients with veggies, salt, chaat masala, cumin powder, and lemon juice.
  4. Add chutneys and curd: Add a spoonful of tamarind chutney, green chutney, and optionally curd. Mix lightly.
  5. Garnish and serve: Top with sev, coriander, and a squeeze of lemon. Serve immediately.
